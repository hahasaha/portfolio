Overview

Through Inkwell, I'm designing internal enterprise tools for a major entertainment company — dashboards and workflow interfaces that help large creative teams move faster and reduce operational friction.

The work spans the full design process: stakeholder research, information architecture, high-fidelity UI, and close collaboration with engineering.

[ Dashboard overview — scenario comparison UI ]

The challenge

Large enterprise teams often deal with tools built for individual contributors that don't scale to cross-functional workflows. The design challenge: create interfaces that are intuitive for experts under pressure, not just novices with time to learn.

[ Workflow detail — violation flags + annotation system ]

My approach

I focused on state legibility above everything else. In high-stakes workflows, users need to know exactly what the system is doing at any moment — what's active, what's changed, what needs their attention.

This shaped every design decision: badge placement, footer action ordering, how the AI assistant surfaces contextual help, and how the map-based interface communicates scale and scope.
