The Problem

The site was leaking every visitor it earned.

90% of traffic was mobile. The site was built for desktop. Walk-in traffic is Golf Ranch's model — no reservations, just show up. But the site wasn't giving people a reason to. No clear CTA above the fold. No lead capture to stay in touch. No local SEO to pull people in when they searched "driving range near me." The site looked like a brochure, not a growth tool.

Lead Generation

The free bucket — a 10,000/month lead machine.

One button. Always visible. Always driving to the free bucket offer. That single decision pulled in 10,000+ leads a month.

Simple idea. The details made it — copy, placement, where the thank-you page took you next. We got all of it right. Rolled it out across all six locations.

The result: over 10,000 new leads per month, consistently.

Membership

4× more members — from one page redesign.

The Ranch Pass membership page was the second major conversion surface. The original buried the value prop — pricing was unclear, there was no urgency, no social proof. I redesigned it from the ground up, leading with the savings math, surfacing testimonials from Golf Ranch's 8,500+ members, and making the sign-up flow frictionless on mobile. Membership signups quadrupled.

Location Pages + SEO

SEO overhaul launched March 2026.

Each Golf Ranch location needed its own SEO-optimized page — structured for local search, consistent in design, and fast to publish as new locations open. I designed a scalable location page template in Webflow that the team can replicate in minutes.

The SEO push launched in March 2026. Organic traffic is already up 25% in the first weeks post-launch.
