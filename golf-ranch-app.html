The Problem

Members were still waiting in line.

Ranch Pass members were still waiting in line at the front desk to buy buckets at their member rate. No app, no self-serve, no loyalty tracking. Golf Ranch needed a product that made membership feel worth it beyond just the price.

What I Designed

Buy a bucket. Get a PIN. Skip the desk.

A mobile app that lets Ranch Pass members skip the line — buy a bucket from their phone, get a PIN or QR code, walk straight to the dispenser. Home screen shows total balls hit, savings, streak, and next milestone. The boarding pass model (full-screen PIN display, high contrast, readable at arm's length) was a key design decision.

Early Wireframes

Stress-testing every edge case.

Before moving to high-fidelity, I mapped all key flows — signup with Snowflake email validation, bucket purchase with Stripe, active pass retrieval, and the milestone tracker. The wireframes stress-tested edge cases: lapsed membership state, failed PIN generation, one-active-pass-at-a-time rule.

The Loyalty System

Four tiers. Every bucket counts.

Four milestone tiers — Range Rookie, Range Regular, Range Rat, Range Legend — each unlocking rewards at 1K, 2.5K, 5K, and 10K balls hit. Streak tracking, per-visit history, and a stats screen that makes progress visible every time you open the app.

"Every bucket earns. Every visit counts."
